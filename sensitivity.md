# How the Sites Perform Under Stress Tests

Stress tests evaluated how each site performs under changes in cap rates, construction costs, and rents. Durham remains the strongest performer in every scenario.

| Scenario | Durham RLV | Glen Allen RLV | Downtown RLV |
|----------|-------------|----------------|----------------|
| **Normal Market** | $56.7M | $38.8M | $38.4M |
| **Cap Rate Increase** | $31.7M | $17.4M | $22.1M |
| **Hard Cost Increase** | $45.8M | $29.9M | $31.3M |
| **Rent Increase** | $65.1M | $45.3M | $43.9M |

### Interpretation

- Durham maintains the highest Residual Land Value (RLV) in **all four scenarios**.  
- Glen Allen and Downtown fall significantly behind under stress, especially when cap rates rise.  
- Durham’s resilience indicates **lower risk** and **stronger long‑term feasibility**.

Resilience protects both public and private investment during market shifts.
