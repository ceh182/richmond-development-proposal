# How the Sites Perform Under Stress Tests

Stress tests evaluated how each site performs under changes in cap rates, construction costs, and rents.

| Scenario | Durham RLV |
|----------|-------------|
| **Normal Market** | $56.7M |
| **Cap Rate Increase** | $31.7M |
| **Hard Cost Increase** | $45.8M |
| **Rent Increase** | $65.1M |

### Interpretation
Durham maintains the highest RLV in every scenario tested. This makes it the **most resilient** site under shifting market conditions.

Resilience protects both public and private investment.
