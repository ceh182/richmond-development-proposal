# Financial Comparison of the Three Sites

The table below summarizes the financial performance of each site under normal market conditions.

| Metric | Durham | Downtown | Glen Allen |
|--------|---------|-----------|-------------|
| **Total Project Value** | $165.6M | $109.1M | $128.6M |
| **Development Cost** | $108.9M | $70.7M | $89.8M |
| **Net Operating Income** | $9.6M | $6.4M | $6.4M |
| **Residual Land Value** | $56.7M | $38.4M | $38.8M |

### Key Takeaways
- **Strongest RLV:** Durham  
- **Highest NOI:** Durham  
- **Most financially resilient:** Durham  

Durham is the only site that combines high value, strong income, and low risk.
