# Recommendation: Advance the Durham Place Development

Based on financial strength, resilience, and public value, **Durham Place is the only defensible site for immediate advancement**.

### Why Durham
- Highest Residual Land Value  
- Most resilient under stress tests  
- Strongest catalytic impact  
- Aligns with Richmond’s long-term growth strategy  

### Why the Other Sites Can Wait
- **Downtown**: Lower project value and uncertain office demand.  
- **Glen Allen**: Financially solid but limited public value; reinforces car-centric sprawl.  

### Market Signal
Advancing Durham signals confidence in the Diamond District and supports Richmond’s young, mixed-use, walkable future.

Scarce capital requires prioritizing the site that maximizes both financial return and public value today.
